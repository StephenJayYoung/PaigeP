  • Dr. Paige Prather

    Combining advanced technology with a gently friendly style Dr. Prather will help you maintain a beautiful healthy smile and whole body health. She continues to expand her knowledge in cosmetic and implant dentistry and offers both oral and IV sedation for patients who have a strong fear of dentist.

    A graduate of Ohio State University, completing a residency at the VA hospital in Dayton Ohio, Dr. Prather has also worked with her father an oral surgeon. She keeps her license in Ohio to train with him on special cases

    Dr. Prather and her husband live in Franklin and have a little boy named Jaxson. They also have a canine child named Lexi. When not practicing dentistry, she enjoys running, rollerblading, biking, a car fanatic for American muscle cars and enjoys riding her Harley!

    Dr. Prather has been a member of the American Dental Association, Nashville Dental Society, Tennessee Dental Association, Academy of General Dentistry, American Dental Society of Anesthesiology and American Academy of Implant Dentistry. Continuing education is of the utmost importance to her. She feels it is the key to prevention. Dentistry is a very young field and is constantly growing and changing. This is why you may receive differing opinions from various dentists. Dr. Prather does her best to stay informed on the most proven, safe and effective treatments for her patients. She strives to treat each patient just as she would treat her own family.

  • Lindsey Calvin - Patient Financial Coordinator

    I am proud and honored to be a part of the such a talented team. Dr. Prather takes a genuine interest in each patient and is devoted to keeping them healthy,relaxed and comfortable. I earned my bachelors degree in art and photography from the University of Kentucky before moving to Nashville in 2011. I really love working with people and helping them achieve their dental health goals and dreams. In my spare time I often travel to Kentucky to see family and friends and also enjoy live music.

  • Kattie Adcock - Scheduling Coordinator

    I am so grateful to be a part of this team. Everyone, including the patients, are so welcoming. I attended TTC in Dickson, TN where I became a Registered Dental Assistant in 2013. I have certifications for Nitrous Oxide Monitoring, Coronal Polishing and Sealants. My husband and I live in White Bluff, TN and we have a spoiled puppy named Ruckus.

  • Chelsea Madison - Registered Dental Hygienist

    It is refreshing and exciting to be working with such an amazing team and a doctor who is as passionate and caring about dentistry as I am. I have extensive training and two certifications in the use of diode and CO2 lasers which expands the options for our patients. It is my goal to go above for each and every patient. I live in Mount Juliet with my husband and three fur babies and one real baby, Christian.
