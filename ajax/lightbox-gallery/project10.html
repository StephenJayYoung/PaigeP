Patient Comfort
At our office, we understand that some of our patients are apprehensive about visits to the dentist. To ease that apprehension, we provide a number of amenities to make your visit with us as comfortable as possible.
  • Nitrous oxide
  • Flatscreen TVs and Music or videos
  • Bose noise-cancelling headphones
  • Soft warm blankets and sleep masks
  • Paraffin Hand Treatments
  • Fresh-baked cookies
  • Coffee
  • Coffee, Tea, and Hot Chocolate
  • Free Wifi
  • Our Caring Staff
